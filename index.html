Welcome to Marcella

A culinary boutique in the heart of Paseo Caribe, San Juan, where creative Creole and international gastronomy meets the beauty of locally sourced ingredients.

Farm-to-Table Excellence

At Marcella, we celebrate Puerto Rico's culinary heritage by partnering with local farmers and producers. Every dish tells a story of fresh, seasonal ingredients transformed through international techniques and creative passion.

Since 2022, we've been dedicated to providing an unforgettable dining experience that blends elegance, warmth, and the authentic flavors of Puerto Rico.

The Violet Orchid Story

Marcella is more than a restaurant—it's a tribute to family, tradition, and the beauty found in nature's details.

The restaurant's name and concept are inspired by the violet orchid, Chef Jose Sanchez's grandmother's favorite flower. Her love for this elegant bloom and her passion for cooking shaped his culinary journey.

Every element of Marcella—from the colors to the plating—reflects the grace and beauty of the violet orchid, creating an experience that honors her memory and celebrates the art of fine dining.
